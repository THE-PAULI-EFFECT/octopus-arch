## 1. Introduction

Octopus Architecture, Inc. ("Octopus," "we," "us," or "our") is a Washington State Social Purpose Corporation that operates a trust-based service marketplace. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share your information.

This Privacy Policy describes our practices concerning information collected through our website, mobile applications, and services (collectively, the "Platform").

**By using our Platform, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.**

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Core Service Operations

- **Provider Verification:** Calculate trust scores, verify business legitimacy
- **Lead Routing:** Connect customers with verified providers
- **Attribution Tracking:** Generate cryptographic hashes for lead attribution
- **Booking Management:** Facilitate booking requests and confirmations
- **Commission Calculation:** Track completed bookings and calculate performance-based fees
- **Social Contribution Tracking:** Monitor and verify community service hours

### 3.2 Platform Improvement

- Analyze usage patterns
- Improve trust algorithms
- Develop new features
- Conduct research and analytics

### 3.3 Communication

- Send service notifications (lead captured, booking confirmed)
- Provide customer support
- Send administrative messages (policy updates, security alerts)
- Marketing communications (with consent, opt-out available)

### 3.4 AI Agent Integration

- Provide structured data to AI agents (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude) via LLMS.txt protocol
- Enable agent-to-platform interactions
- Share trust scores and provider information with agents

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 With Service Providers (for Customers)

When you submit a lead, we share your contact information with the selected provider so they can respond to your request.

### 4.2 With Customers (for Providers)

Your business information (name, services, description, trust score) is displayed publicly to help customers find and evaluate you.

### 4.3 With AI Agents

Verified provider information is shared with AI agents via our LLMS.txt protocol. This includes:
- Business name and description
- Services offered
- Trust score
- Geographic coverage
- Contact information (business phone/email only)

### 4.4 With Service Partners

We share information with:
- **Supabase** (database hosting)
- **Cloudflare** (CDN and security)
- **Firecrawl** (business verification)
- **DocuSeal** (contract generation)
- **Payment processors** (Stripe, etc.)

All partners are contractually obligated to protect your data.

### 4.5 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information:
- To comply with laws, regulations, or legal process
- To enforce our Terms of Service
- To protect rights, safety, or property
- In connection with corporate transactions (merger, sale)

### 4.6 With Your Consent

We may share information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Provider Data:** Retained while account is active, plus 7 years after closure (for tax/legal compliance)
- **Customer Leads:** Retained for 2 years after lead creation (for attribution disputes)
- **Trust Score Data:** Retained indefinitely (for fraud prevention)
- **Booking Records:** Retained for 7 years (for tax/legal compliance)
- **Social Contribution Data:** Retained indefinitely (public benefit documentation)

You may request deletion of certain data (see Section 8).

---

## 6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption:** TLS/SSL for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, principle of least privilege
- **Authentication:** Strong password requirements, optional 2FA
- **Database Security:** Row-level security (Supabase RLS)
- **Monitoring:** Real-time security monitoring and alerts

**However, no system is 100% secure. Use strong passwords and protect your credentials.**

### 7.1 Access and Correction
